Document Redaction Demo

Identifying the parts of the document which need to be redacted.

3 Methods are being considered:
  1. Keywords Finding specific words
  2. Entity Recognition Setting specific rules for types of entities. For example, redact every organization name, or names of people.
  3. Context-based redaction through machine learning, analyze what a human did, and attempt to re-create it
More data is required to properly train the model (before and after redaction)
