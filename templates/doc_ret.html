Proposed methodology:
  1. Gather documents
  2. Perform OCR (must be able to accept multiple formats: PDF, scanned PDF, TIFF, JPEG, etc.
  3. Compare OCR to query (ATIP request in this case)
    • Document vs document
    • Sentence by sentence (displaying with a histogram, perhaps?)
    • Could also use an unsupervised model (LDA)
  4. Rank documents
Much of this is applicable to other contexts, such as discovery in a legal setting
